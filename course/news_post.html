Sri Ramachandra Institute of Higher Education and Research

SRET was established in 2019 Currently offers BTech, BSc, MSc, and PhD Programs Bachelors Programs focus on: Strong fundamentals and the engineering skills needed in industry 50% of the learning time is to master the theory and concepts while the balance is for application and practice Mandatory internship every year as part of experiential learning Conducive environment for students to be creative and innovative Industry and International collaboration for learning and applied research Masters Programs focus on skills enhancement, advanced learning and applied research Doctoral Programs focus on applied and translational research Doctoral Programs offer options for scholarsâ€™ work to be at international labs or institutions for up to 18 months Our Mission to promote excellence in engineering and technology education to prepare graduates to become technology leaders in industry, business and education to be at the forefront of innovation and applied research.

The aim of the SRET, for the individual student, is to eliminate the need in his life for the college; the task is to help him become a self-educating man.

Sri Ramachandra Institute of Higher Education and Research

  • Sri Ramachandra Medical College and Research Institute was founded in 1985 by the Founder Chancellor, Shri. N.P.V. Ramasamy Udayar
    • Attained Deemed University status in 1994 Renamed as Sri Ramachandra University in 2006 In 2018, Sri Ramachandra was recognized as Category-1 University by UGC In 2019, SRIHER was ranked as the 33rd University by NIRF Attained CGPA of 3.62 out of 4.0 from NAAC, the highest among all private medical institutions in the country India Today survey recognized SRIHER as the best private Health Sciences University in the country The Medical Council of India (MCI) has recognized SRIHER as a Nodal Centre for medical education technology courses The World Health Organization has designated the SRMC & RI as a WHO Collaborating Centre for Research and Training in Occupational Health Atomic Energy Regulatory Board (AERB) has accredited the Biodosimetry lab for assessment of personnel radiation exposure & related studies ICMR has recognized SRIHER Public Health as ICMR Centre for Advanced Research on Air Quality, Climate and Health Sri Ramachandra Engineering and Technology was established in 2019 as the 14th Faculty of SRIHER Dr. P.V. Vijayaraghavan is the current Vice Chancellor of the University Shri. V.R. Venkataachalam, Chancellor and Shri. R.V. Sengutuvan, Pro Chancellor lead the University which has more than 7000 students
